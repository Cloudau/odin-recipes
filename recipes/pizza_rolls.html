Pizza Rolls Recipe

Description

A Recipe for Pizza Rolls. It is a delicious dish and tastes like a Pizza.

Ingredients

  • 500g of flour
  • 15g of salt + some extra
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 pack of instant yeast (enough for 500g)
  • ca. 250ml lukewarm water
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 350g of pureed tomatoes
  • 75g vegan cheese
  • optional: olives, smoked tofu, pineapple, cherry tomatoes...

Steps

  1. Mix flour and 15g of salt in a big bowl.
  2. Push a mould into the center of the flour and add yeast and sugar. Mix with a little bit of lukewarm water.
  3. Cover and let rest for five minutes.
  4. After five minutes, the yeast should be bubbly. Add the rest of the water and the oil and knead until you get a nice smooth dough. You might need a little extra flour or water to get a perfectly smooth, dough.
  5. Cover dough and allow to rest for 30 minutes.
  6. You can use the time to cut up whatever you want to fill the rolls with: for example olives, pineapple, smoked tofu or cherry tomatoes...
  7. Preheat oven to 200째C/390째F.
  8. Roll out dough to a rectangle, not too thick (2-3mm)
  9. Set aside about 50ml of the pureed tomatoes. Spread the rest on the dough.
  10. Lightly salt the tomatoes.
  11. Now add the "stuffing", in my case black olives.
  12. The next step is rolling up the dough. I try to push down on the sides of the dough, so the sauce doesn't seep out.
  13. Once your done rolling, cut slices and put then on a baking tray.
  14. Once on the baking tray, add the rest of the tomatoes on top, as well as vegan cheese.
  15. Bake at 200째C/390째F for about 22 Minuten.
  16. Done! :)
